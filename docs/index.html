Andrew M Rose

Hi | Bonjour | Kwe’

I’m Andrew Rose and I’m a Learning Manager of Math and Physics at Holland College on Prince Edward Island. I teach at both the Summerside Harbourfront and Charlottetown, Prince of Wales Campuses. Prior to joining Holland College I worked with Health PEI as a Health Information Specialist performing statistical analysis and data visualization of data related to the province’s healthcare services. As well, I was an Instructor at the College of the North Atlantic in Carbonear, Newfoundland. There I taught post-secondary mathematics within the CAS Transfer Program. I hold a BSc (Joint Hons) in Applied Math and Physics, an MSc in Math, and a BEd in Intermediate/Secondary Education. Please take a look at my CV for a more detailed history.

This site is intended to be a home for course resources, professional data projects, and personal projects which may be data based or otherwise. Please feel free to contact me regarding any errors to the website, or with any questions for the published projects!
